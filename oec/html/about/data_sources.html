Data Sources

All of the trade data shown on the site is either in the SITC (Standard International Trade Classification) or HS (Harmonized System) four digit level. We aggregate to the 4 digit level to reduce the visual complexity of the visualizations used throughout the site (where screen real-estate is at such a premium).

SITC

For historical SITC classification data (1962 - 2000), we use data from The Center for International Data from Robert Feenstra1. For more recent data (2001 - 2010), we use data provided by UN COMTRADE.

HS

For all of the HS data used throughout the site (1995 - 2011) we use the BACI International Trade Database also at the 4 digit level2. The original data comes from the United Nations Statistical Division (COMTRADE database) but is cleaned by the BACI team using their own methodology of harmonization.

References

  1. Feenstra, R. C., et al. (2005). World Trade Flows, 1962–2000. NBER working paper 11040
